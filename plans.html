BOOK RENTAL PLANS

Basic

This plan suits individuals who enjoy reading at their own pace and may not finish books quickly.

Features:

  • Rent one book at a time.
  • Affordable pricing for occassional readers.
  • Easy renewal options.

Standard

This plan targets avid readers who go through books more quickly and may want access to a broader selection.

Features:

  • Rent multiple book simultaneously.
  • Higher book-per-month limit.
  • Prior access to new releases or exclusive titles.

Family

Tailored for families or households with multiple readers, the family plan offers a cost-effective way to share books among family members.

Features:

  • Multiple simultaneous rentals for family members.
  • Shared library access.
  • Flexible rental durations.
